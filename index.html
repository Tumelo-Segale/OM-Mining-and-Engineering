Company Description

Who We Are

We are a privately owned company that was established in 2012 with the sole mandate of providing strategic and integrated development solutions within South Africa and beyond.

Overview

Obed Moleti Construction and Projects (Pty) Ltd is a privately owned, predominantly Black male-owned company established in 2012 at Chaneng Village in Rustenburg. We operate mainly as a supplier within South Africa and beyond, with a sole mandate of providing integrated development and strategic solutions to the established and emerging corporate market.

Our core approach is to provide effective business services by consolidating our current knowledge and unit standards in terms of our principal service offer.

Vision

To be one of the leading companies recognized for quality work, service delivery and client satisfaction - attaining our objectives and goals to be sustainable, with a listing on the JSE as our main goal.

Mission Statement

We are committed to employment creation, acceleration of B-BBEE participation, and industrial and economic development, while we advance as an emerging SMME 441 through the provision and facilitation of business growth, trade and investment opportunities.
